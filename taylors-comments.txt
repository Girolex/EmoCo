// novice level
- What’s the difference between melody and harmony?
- What makes some chords sound happy and others sound sad?

// adept level
- How can you make a rhythm feel like it’s “pushing forward” or “relaxing”?
- What kind of chord progressions are common in lo-fi tracks?

// professional level
- Design a full arrangement that evolves emotionally across three sections without changing tempo.
- Describe a system for mapping emotion to musical parameters (key, register, dynamics) that could guide composition.



// general
- Compile a playlist of sea shanties throughout history and through the modern day.
